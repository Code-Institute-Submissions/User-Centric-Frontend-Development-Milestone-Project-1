About:

The story

The 'Cottage Tea Rooms' were established in 2014 by Antoinette Kelly and her husband Michael. In this family business they recreated the cultural Irish Tea rooms and brought the experience to the modern world. The building was bought and restored from a simple cottage to a cafe with a cozy and timely design.
